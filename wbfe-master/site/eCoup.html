eCoup - A Digital Revolution

eCoup is a digital revolution to overthrow predatory capitalism and rent seekers. The eCoup Digital Village will connect all White Buffalo Co-op Patrons (members), and provide a socially responsible network for cooperation and prosperity. eCoup will bridge the digital divide using blockchain technology, networks and specialized training to leapfrog legacy systems and social networks and connect the unconnected.

Indigenous populations worldwide total 300 million people. Despite symbolic representation at the UN and national levels, tribes face technical and political challenges communicating, cooperating and organizing at the global level. This lack of connection has created a digital divide, where those not digitally connected are at a disadvantage. Indigenous lands are often exploited as a result of this divide, leading to deforestation, mining and other activities that harm tribal lands and the global environment. This divide also leads to poverty. The U.S. Center for Disease Control identifies internet access, mobile phone access, social network access, healthcare, education, poverty and our natural environment as critical Social Determinants of Health.

eCoup Goals


- Promote White Buffalo Co-op membership to all and provide outreach and free membership to unbanked and unconnected indigenous tribes.

- Provide an online community for Co-op Patrons to connect, launch and participate in Co-op businesses and community development projects, vote on Co-op issues, learn, exchange assets and build a sustainable, profitable community with global influence.

- Support and fund efforts to protect tribal lands and the environment, particularly where a lack of resources has resulted in exploitation of tribal lands.

- Transition retail, commercial and government payments and banking transactions to distributed ledger technologies providing security, safety, speed and savings.

- Provide vocational training, educational resources and jobs, particularly in blockchain, information technology and farming.

- Offer financial services, credit facilities, market data and global market access for Reserve-backed, tribally owned credit programs and businesses.

- Provide last mile access to the technical resources necessary for social health like internet connectivity and mobile phone service throughout tribal lands.

- Provide online access to telemedicine and e-Governance Services like voting, tax remittance, vehicle registration and emergency services to global tribal communities.

- Maintain a blockchain powered Archive for tribal culture, science and history to save and share history, traditions, values, legends and knowledge.

About us

White Buffalo Co-op Patrons are building an ethical and healthy ecosystem to launch businesses, create financial opportunity, protect privacy and guarantee social welfare using digital assets and ensuring ownership and voting rights for all Patrons.
