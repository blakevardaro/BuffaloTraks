Why Join Us?

White Buffalo Co-op Patrons are building an ethical and healthy ecosystem to launch businesses, create financial opportunity, protect privacy and guarantee social welfare using digital assets and ensuring ownership and voting rights for all Patrons. A unique, trackable SELF>TRAK$ membership certificate is issued to Patrons who have read the Co-op Whitepaper, agreed to the By Laws and provided consent to contribute effort to the White Buffalo Co-op. SELF>TRAK$ are a blockchain-based authentication and validation of identity and credit ownership.

The White Buffalo Co-op combines a Reserve, Credit Program, TRAK$ credits, and eCoup. The Co-op is in the process of launching multiple TRAK$ based businesses. Using this transparent, open source, democratized platform, all Patrons have ownership and can vote on Co-op and related business issues. TRAK$ are legally Co-op asset-backed credits and not securities. As a sovereign agency, the Reserve works with governments, businesses, consumers and all stakeholders to create regulations and settle disputes in industries where these protections do not currently exist, eliminating the lack of recourse common in digital asset disputes. Early patrons of White Buffalo Co-op will receive special considerations. Patrons have a variety of ways to contribute to the Co-op, including, but not limited to, starting a TRAK$ based business, referring new Patrons or contributing to the development of TRAK$.

As a Community Development Entity, the Reserve contributes 58% of profits to development projects in low income and under-served indigenous communities and projects that help save our shared natural environment. Our Reserve is charged with providing credit and financial services to all Patrons, including the 1.7 Billion unbanked. What makes the White Buffalo Co-op unique is our ability to offer earning potential to the 75% of the unbanked who currently have no assets to deposit. Our Credit Program also partners with local indigenous people to launch their own local Credit Programs. Join the White Buffalo Co-op now and help build a sustainable, reserve-backed credit and business ecosystem that ensures privacy, financial security and eliminates rent seekers.
