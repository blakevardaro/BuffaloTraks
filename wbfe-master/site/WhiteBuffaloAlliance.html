The Alliance combines the power of consumers, businesses, developers and entrepreneurs to promote the use of TRAK$. The Alliance funds new businesses that use TRAK$ and provides training and jobs for members.

The White Buffalo Alliance is a US Department of Treasury certified Community Development Entity, and contributes 56% of profits to community development projects in low-income and under-served communities and to projects that help save our shared natural environment.
