Welcome to the White Buffalo Developer Portal!


This site is designed to aid those interested in developing applications on the White Buffalo blockchain.

The code for this site is on github. If you’d like to see a specific improvement you can either submit a pull request or file an issue. Please follow our contribution guidelines, please keep in mind the following:

  • Everyone is welcome to contribute code to White Buffalo, LCA. Projects. We have a Code of Conduct; we ask that you please follow it in all your interactions with our team and your fellow contributors.

  • We are a Limited Cooperative Association and as such we require that members contribute to community. If you are a member of the White Buffalo LCA and are interested in open source development on White Buffalo, LCA. Please inform us of that fact on your pull requests to tha we can count your time and effort toward your contribution and surplus of HODL>TRAK$ credits.

Our tutorials section will take you through the basics of interacting with our APIs as they are developed. More detailed API specs will Join thevailable as we reach certain milestones. Those apis are under development, so please read notes at the top of each section if you are given access to a back-end project.

You can find community resources, libraries, and more ‘human’ ways to get help and education in the communities section as it develops, please be patient.

About us

White Buffalo Co-op Patrons are building an ethical and healthy ecosystem to launch businesses, create financial opportunity, protect privacy and guarantee social welfare using digital assets and ensuring ownership and voting rights for all Patrons.
