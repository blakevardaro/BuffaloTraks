TRAK$

Currency for healthy communities.


TRAK$ are a stable, reserve-backed, regulated digital currency issued by a US Department of Treasury certified Community Development Entity called the White Buffalo Alliance. TRAK$ save time and money and protect your data. 56% of profits fund community development and environmental programs. TRAK$ are free to use and any business can accept them.
